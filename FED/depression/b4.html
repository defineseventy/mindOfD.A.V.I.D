Option B: Talk to Someone

“Enough is enough.” He went to his parents and shared what was going on, his thoughts and struggles.

Surprisingly, his parents were very understanding and they told him,

“Look, what others think of you might not always be what you truly are. There are other things that you are good at. Take your time to find out your strengths and weaknesses, don’t let the opinions of others define who you are.”

The next day, they went to school to speak with his teachers about the bullying from his CCA members.
