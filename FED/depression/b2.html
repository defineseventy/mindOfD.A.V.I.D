Option B: Overthinking, man up instead

David tried, really, to stop letting things get to him. He is only overthinking.

“Stop being a party-pooper when everyone is gathered here having fun.”

Despite how tired he feels, he continued hanging out with his friends.

David should just be a man and deal with this. There are so much more people out there suffering worse than him, he should stop pitying himself.
